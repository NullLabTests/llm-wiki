**Persistent personal knowledge bases powered by LLMs — beyond RAG.**

Traditional RAG forces your LLM to rediscover everything on every single query.  
**LLM Wiki** turns your LLM into a tireless **knowledge engineer** that reads raw documents once and maintains a clean, interlinked, ever-evolving Markdown second brain.

**Goal**: Build a living second brain that *accumulates* refined knowledge instead of starting from scratch.

### Quick Start (30 seconds)

1. Drop your documents/notes/PDFs into the `raw/` folder  
2. Copy the content of `llm-wiki.md`  
3. Paste it into Claude / GPT / Grok and say:

> **"You are my LLM Wiki engineer. Build and maintain the wiki from the documents in raw/."**

### 2026 State-of-the-Art Enhancements

- Agentic Memory (AgeMem)
