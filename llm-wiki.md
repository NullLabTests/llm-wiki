# LLM Wiki

Copy-paste this file into your LLM agent.

## Core Idea
Standard RAG = rediscover knowledge every query. No accumulation.  
**LLM Wiki** = LLM becomes a knowledge engineer that builds and maintains a living Markdown knowledge base from your raw documents.

- Raw files stay in `/raw/` (immutable)  
- Refined, linked knowledge lives in `/wiki/`

## How to Use
1. Create `raw/` and `wiki/` folders  
2. Put your materials in `raw/`  
3. Paste this file into your LLM and say:  
   > "You are my LLM Wiki engineer. Build and maintain the wiki from the documents in raw/ using the pattern above."
